About This App

The Medicinal Herbal App is an educational resource designed to help users learn about common medicinal herbs, their traditional uses, and important safety information.

What We Provide

  • Comprehensive information about medicinal herbs
  • Benefits and traditional uses
  • Preparation methods and dosage recommendations
  • Important safety warnings and contraindications
  • Drug interaction information
  • Search functionality by herb name, benefit, or plant family

Important Safety Information

⚠️ MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

This application is for EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult with qualified healthcare providers before:

  • Using any herbal remedies
  • Starting any herbal supplement
  • Combining herbs with prescription medications
  • Using herbs if you have existing medical conditions
  • Using herbs during pregnancy or breastfeeding

Key Facts About Herbs

  • ✓ Herbs can have serious interactions with medications
  • ✓ Herbs may cause allergic reactions or side effects
  • ✓ Dosages matter - more is not always better
  • ✓ Quality and safety vary by source and preparation
  • ✓ Regulation of herbal supplements varies by country
  • ✓ Individual reactions to herbs can vary significantly

Before Using Any Herb

Safety Checklist:
  • ☐ Tell your doctor about ALL herbs you plan to use
  • ☐ Inform your doctor of all medications you take
  • ☐ Check for allergies or sensitivities first
  • ☐ Start with small amounts to test tolerance
  • ☐ Buy from reputable, quality sources only
  • ☐ Stop use if you experience any adverse effects
  • ☐ Never replace medical treatment with herbs alone
  • ☐ Pregnant/breastfeeding? Get doctor approval first

When to Seek Medical Help

Stop using herbs and seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

  • Allergic reactions (rash, swelling, difficulty breathing)
  • Severe nausea, vomiting, or stomach pain
  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Dizziness or severe headaches
  • Any unusual or concerning symptoms
